About me

Hi! My name is Maike Anthony, and I'm a two-time Guinness World Memory Record holder[1][2]. After teaching memory and study methods for over 5 years on my YouTube channel, I've noticed many people felt the Memory Palace method required too much effort to be applied often, and many struggled to follow the step-by-step procedure. Hence, I built this website[3] to help anyone apply the technique easily and systematically.
